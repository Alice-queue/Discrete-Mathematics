  \item For a relation $R$ on $\mathbf{Z}$,
  define $aRb$ if there is postive integer $r$ such that $a = b^r$.
  Prove that R is a partial ordering of $\mathbf{Z}$.

  \item Prove:
  $
  \forall a, b \in S, a\prec b \implies (a\precsim b \land a \neq b)
  $
