Idempotent laws: 1. $x + x = x$; 2. $x \cdot x = 0$.

Proof 1:

\begin{eqnarray*}
x + x & = & (x + x)\cdot 1 \\
& = & (x + x)\cdot (x + x') \\
& = & x + (x \cdot x') \\
& = & x + 0 \\
& = & x
\end{eqnarray*}

Key: $x + (x \cdot x') = (x + x) \cdot (x + x')$
