Idempotent laws: 1. $x + x = x$; 2. $x * x = 0$.

Proof 1:

\begin{eqnarray*}
x + x & = & (x + x) * 1 \\
& = & (x + x) * (x + x') \\
& = & x + (x * x') \\
& = & x + 0 \\
& = & x
\end{eqnarray*}

Key: $x + (x * x') = (x + x) * (x + x')$

Proof 2:

\begin{eqnarray*}
x * x & = & (x * x) + 0 \\
& = & (x * x) + (x * x') \\
& = & x * (x + x') \\
& = & x * 1 \\
& = & x
\end{eqnarray*}
